who I am

Hi! My name is Anastasiia.

I create modern and responsive websites using html, css, and js.

In my work, I use AI for design and partly for code optimization to complete tasks faster and more efficiently.

I enjoy making websites not only convenient for users but also those that convey the mood, atmosphere, and style of the brand. I’m constantly learning to notice details and make everything look harmonious.

I also love creating interactive websites, so in the future, I plan to build even more engaging projects using 3D models.

In addition, I want to learn web design, React, and other programming languages to keep expanding my skills and make my websites even more impressive
